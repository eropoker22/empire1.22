# Empire Streets Monetization Roadmap

Empire Streets public alpha starts with Free mode. Free mode remains the public, free alpha/fun-test path for first players.

## War Pass

- War Pass is planned for a later phase as a one-time account unlock while the service operates.
- Target price is approximately 9.99 EUR / 10 EUR.
- War Pass must not be pay-to-win.
- War mode must not be sold until production persistence, production session security, and War balance are validated.
- War mode stays founders / closed beta until the server, map, and balance are ready.

## Cosmetics

- Premium avatars are planned as cosmetic-only items.
- Cosmetic purchases must not change income, combat, cooldowns, police pressure, faction strength, map control, or matchmaking.
- Copy must avoid implying power or gameplay advantage.

## Custom Avatars

- Custom avatar generation from a player photo is planned only after privacy, consent, moderation, and deletion workflows exist.
- Do not add a photo upload endpoint before those workflows are ready.
- Avoid wording such as "forever" unless the product clearly explains account access duration and service availability.

## War Map Requirement

War mode targets 150 players. It must not launch as a paid public mode on the current 161-district Free map, because that is roughly 1.07 districts per player. War needs either a larger validated map or a substantially different design before public sale.
